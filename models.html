IBSS Models Layer

The conceptual engines behind the platform. These models convert signals into structure, scenarios into logic, and analysis into sovereign decision posture.

Σ-9X
Sovereign Intelligence Protocol
The core analytical protocol of IBSS. Σ-9X structures how signals are received, assessed, and transformed into strategic interpretation.
WAAD
War Acceptance Architecture Doctrine
A model for understanding how wars are made politically acceptable before they begin operationally. It detects narrative construction, legitimacy transfer, and pre-war conditioning.
DLM
Diplomatic Legitimacy Matrix
A framework for measuring the political legitimacy environment around negotiations, mediation, and diplomatic positioning.
LDE
Leader Decision Engine
A model focused on leadership choice architecture: pressure, timing, signaling, and thresholds that shape top-level decisions.
How the Models Work Together
IBSS does not treat models as isolated labels. Each model represents one layer of the decision environment:
  • Σ-9X defines the overall sovereign logic of the system.
  • WAAD explains how conflict becomes politically viable.
  • DLM maps diplomatic space and legitimacy pressure.
  • LDE interprets leadership behavior inside compressed crisis moments.
Why This Matters
Most analysis explains events after they happen. IBSS models are designed to structure what is forming before it becomes obvious.

The purpose is not prediction in the simplistic sense, but disciplined signal interpretation inside a sovereign analytical architecture.
